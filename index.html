About me

I'm Hector Bell, a 23 year old writer/director and graduate from the University for the Creative Arts Farnham.

My main aspiration is to make films that could be used to provoke some form of social change, as I believe it's important to use the medium in such a way that provokes thought and highlights the unspoken.
