Coming to Light

Coming to Light is the story of 21 year-old Aaron who is struggling to come forward about the sexual abuse he suffered as a child, partly because this secret has manifested itself as a terrifying creature.

Coming to Light is currently being submitted to film festivals, a screener copy can be provided on request.
